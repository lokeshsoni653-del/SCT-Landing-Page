Who We Are

Bridging The Rural-Digital Divide

Founded by students for students, Students Club Tharparkar has grown from a local support circle in Mithi into a dedicated district-wide network addressing education inequality.

Our Mission

To make modern digital literacy, career advice, and leadership building tools accessible and free of charge for every student, volunteer, and girl in the Tharparkar desert region.

Our Vision

A self-sustaining hub of rural IT professionals, digital creators, and visionary leaders coming from the desert schools to contribute to international tech platforms.

Our Core Pillars

Focusing on three core principles: **Educational Support** (IT, guidance), **Leadership Grooming** (debates, speaker bootcamps), and **Youth Welfare** (scholarships).

Why We Do What We Do

Tharparkar is rich in student potential, yet lacks computer hardware access, mentor networks, and career counsel. SCT acts as a critical link. We gather resources, invite top field engineers, and establish makeshift tech camps right in the desert hubs.

  • Empowering girls through digital outreach initiatives
  • Distributing local scholarships to deserving desert students
  • Incubating rural software creators and freelancers
